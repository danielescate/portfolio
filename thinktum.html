thinktum

Graphic Designer

At thinktum, I played a pivotal role in designing key pages for our interim site, collaborating closely with developers. During the rebrand, I contributed by creating icons, visuals, background videos, and photo edits for the revamped website and social media platforms. My background in user experience and interface design further allowed me to collaborate with the UX/UI Designer to develop responsive screens for an insurance web app. Using Figma, I crafted desktop and mobile screens, placeholder illustrations, and animated loading screens, significantly improving the overall user experience and visual appeal. Through these efforts, we successfully enhanced usability, engaged users, and maintained a competitive edge in a fast-changing industry.
