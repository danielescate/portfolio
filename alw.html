Avery Lane Wellness

This is a wellness brand I helped launch back in 2018. With a moodboard provided from the client, I was able to designed the logo and develop the overall look and feel of the brand. In 2020, I helped with re-branding to fit thier current asthetic, as well as, develop a web-based program.
The goal of the program was to allow users, who have purchased the online workout program, to follow the workout routine with images and timers that change after each timer/workout move has finished.
I also assisted with setting up a payment process. Using Memberful, my client is able to rreceive payments through Stripe, view who has signed up for their program, and send them a welcome package through email as soon as they sign up!
