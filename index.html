ABOUT ME

Hi!

I am currently attending George Brown College to study Interaction Design and Development.
I have always been a creative individual with a strong interest in understanding how things work and how they can be improved through innovative design.
As a kid, I enjoyed watching my siblings play video games more than playing them myself because I was fascinated, yet curious, about the different approaches everyone took. My current goal is to create intuitive designs that lead to seamless user experiences, no matter the medium.
I have had the opportunity to work in a variety of different positions which have helped me gain the skills that I confidently possess today and apply in my portfolio work.
