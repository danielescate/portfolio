Oxford Properties Group

Graphic Design
2025


Branding



As part of an offsite team event in Texas, Oxford Properties Group requested a t-shirt design including silhouettes of properties they own around the world. Using Illustrator and Photoshop, I created vectors of the buildings and added elements to give more of a western feeling to the design.

The front design incorperates the logo combined with 2 intertwined horseshoes symbolizing how the two teams are coming together.

An alternative design was also created to contrast the other. This one was more hyper-realistic with a distressed look.
