EdCan Network

After doing freelance work with EdCan for a year, I was brought on as an Intern during my final semester at George Brown College and upon graduating, was promoted to a Junior Graphic Designer/Digital Content Publisher. I designed and helped with the launch of 4 websites: the Well at Work site that hosts articles/ressources, the BC Network site, a wellbeing course site, and a coaching program platform for education leaders. I have also created a variety of assest such as logos, advertisements, icons, posters/infographics, magazine advertisements, and work booklets for educators. I have also learned how to manage their WordPress site, SEO tagging, scheduling social content through Hootsuite, and using MailChimp to design and send out email campaings.
EdCan has helped me strengthen my skills and allowed me to learn new skills.
