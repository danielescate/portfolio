Best Worst News Network (BWNN)

Overview

An interactive video that is controlled by the viewer. In this project, the viewer will direct the flow of the news broadcast and how well (or poorly) it is executed. News anchors have assistance from people in the control room.

In this scenario, the viewer is the only person in the control room. They full power on what to cut to next (ex. news story to weather) and how well that portion goes (ex. sending wrong information to the news anchor). The viewer plays a crucial part in the interactive video. It is entirely up to the viewer if they want to see a good or very bad news broadcast.
