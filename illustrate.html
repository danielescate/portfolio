Branding

Logo

To modernize the illustrate inc logo, a new font was chosen, a refreshed color pallete added, and a brand new icon to top it off, visualizing the i's.

Typography

Moving away from a single font, the new illustrate inc uses Basic Sans for bold titles that have some character. Paired with Articulat CF for body text, this new combo is visually appealing and allows for easy readability.

Colour

The primary color was lightened to evoke feelings of calmness and productivity. To contrast, a dark navy was added to give the feeling of reliability and sophistication. Together, these colours give a sense of trust.

Iconography

The main icons visualizes two people who have each others backs, connecting to slogan "We have you covered.". Plus a custom set of icons used throughout the new website and in illustrate inc's updated insurance system.
