Trans-Co

Overview

Trans-Co is a concept video for a mobile application that allows transit riders reserve a seat and see how full buses are. With COVID spreading and physical distancing measures in-place, many transit buses had to reduce the amount of passengers allowed on.

This led to some people (including me) getting left behind during rush hours. Trans-Co aims to solve this issue with a mobile application that connects passengers with drivers.

This video was created in an IG Story format.
